A five-letter anagram puzzle, one a day.

HowManyAnagrams is a word puzzle for iPhone. Each puzzle shows five letters; you rearrange them, bank the words you find, and finish to see how many of the accepted anagrams you found. It draws from a finite, curated collection of more than 300 puzzles.

There are no timers, streaks, scores, or badges. It is meant to feel calm and unhurried, a quiet puzzle to return to rather than a race.

The app works fully offline, including in airplane mode. There is no account, no subscription, and no tracking.
